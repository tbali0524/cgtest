Are secret messages hidden in famous texts?

Search the given [[TEXT]] for evenly spaced letters which form [[WORD]]s from the given list. Ignore punctuation, capitalization, and spacing.

Print the section of [[TEXT]] that contains the longest hidden [[WORD]], in lowercase and omitting all non-letters. Highlight the hidden [[WORD]] with UPPERCASE. There will be only one such longest word.
