At an [[H]]x[[W]] rave, Bob wants to kiss as many girls as possible without his odds of catching monkeypox exceeding 40%.  The chances of catching monkeypox from any given girl at position (x,y) are  min(x, y) / (x^2 + y^2 + 1).
