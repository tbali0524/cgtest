Recognize the shape made up of {{#}} and give the coordinates of the corners

The possible shapes are:
* <<POINT>>
* <<LINE>>
* <<EMPTY TRIANGLE>>
* <<FILLED TRIANGLE>>
* <<EMPTY SQUARE>>
* <<FILLED SQUARE>>
* <<EMPTY RECTANGLE>>
* <<FILLED RECTANGLE>>

Coordinates: 
* (0,0) is top left
* (19,0) is top right
* (0,19) is bottom left
* (19,19) is bottom right

Medium
